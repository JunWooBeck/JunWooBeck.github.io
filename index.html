Jun Woo Beck

M.S. student in Music Technology · Music Informatics Group, Georgia Tech

I am an M.S. student in Music Technology at Georgia Tech, working in the Music Informatics Group. My research applies machine learning and music information retrieval to sound effects and music audio.

Before Georgia Tech, I studied Electronic Production and Design at Berklee College of Music and worked in Seoul as a recording/mixing engineer, string arranger, and violinist.
