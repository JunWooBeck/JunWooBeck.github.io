Jun Woo Beck

M.S. student in Music Technology · Music Informatics Group, Georgia Tech

I am a second-year Master’s student in Music Technology at Georgia Tech, working in the Music Informatics Group. My research interests include machine learning and music information retrieval (MIR) for sound effects and music audio, multimodal learning across audio, video, and language, and audio dataset construction.

I am working on my master’s thesis — multimodal music recommendation for video — and on interactive performances that connect an audience in real time using their own biosignals.
