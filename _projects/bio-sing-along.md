Bio Sing-Along converts galvanic skin response (GSR) and photoplethysmography
(PPG) biosensor data from the audience into interactive musical elements
during live performance, turning listeners' biosignals into a live musical
dialogue with the performer.

In collaboration with [Eun Ji Oh](https://eunjioh.com/) (project lead).
Supported by a GTCMT Seed Grant; premiered May 2, 2026 at *PRISM Pt. 1:
Sound Through New Lens*, Georgia Tech Center for Music Technology.
