What if your body could sing back? Bio Sing-Along converts galvanic skin
response (GSR) and photoplethysmography (PPG) biosensor data into
interactive musical elements during live performance.

With [Eun Ji Oh](https://eunjioh.com/). Premiered May 2, 2026 at *PRISM
Pt. 1: Sound Through New Lens*, Georgia Tech Center for Music Technology.
